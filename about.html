Cleanvo Care Solution

A home hygiene brand built for everyday life

We started Cleanvo with a simple belief: cleaning products should be dependable, pleasant to use, and honest about what they do.

Our story

Why Cleanvo exists

Every household needs cleaning essentials that work without drama. Too often, shelves are filled with loud claims and confusing choices. We wanted something clearer — products people can pick up with confidence and use as part of a normal day.

Cleanvo began in Shahjahanpur with that practical mindset. We focus on home hygiene done right: clean floors, fresh living spaces, and guidance that is easy to follow.

Our mission

Premium with simplicity

Our mission is to be Your Everyday Hygiene Partner — not by promising the impossible, but by getting the everyday details right: fragrance that feels fresh, usage that is straightforward, and packaging that explains what you need to know.

We are growing carefully, starting with Floor Cleaner and expanding into the essentials Indian homes use every week.
