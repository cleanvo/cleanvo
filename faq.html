Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about Cleanvo products, safe use, and availability. Still unsure? Reach us anytime.

Product usage

Dilute the recommended amount in a bucket of water as shown on the pack, mop the floor evenly, then allow it to dry. For regular use there is no separate rinse step — always follow your bottle label if it differs.

Floor Cleaner is intended for most common household floors. If you have a delicate or specialty surface, test a small hidden area first and follow pack guidance. When unsure, contact us before regular use.

More is not always better. Use the dilution recommended on the pack for everyday mopping. Over-concentrating can leave residue and wastes product.

Safety & storage

Cleanvo products are for household cleaning when used as directed. Keep bottles away from children, do not mix with other cleaners unless the pack allows it, and read caution notes on the label. See our Quality & Safety page for more detail.

Store upright, tightly closed, in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight and food items. Keep in the original labelled container — do not pour into unmarked bottles used for drinks or cooking.
