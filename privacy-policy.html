2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through this static marketing website, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to visitors who browse the site or submit an enquiry through our Contact form. It does not cover third-party websites you may visit via links from our pages.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

When you use the Contact form, you may provide:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • City
  • Message / enquiry content

You may also contact us directly by phone or email. In those cases, we process the information you choose to share with us.

3.2 Information collected automatically

Like most websites, technical data such as browser type, device type, and general usage patterns may be processed by hosting or third-party tools we enable (for example fonts or map embeds). We do not run a custom backend login or store shopper accounts on this site.

4. How we use your information

We use enquiry information to:

  • Respond to customer, retailer, or distributor questions
  • Provide product or availability information you requested
  • Improve how we communicate about Cleanvo products
  • Meet basic legal or compliance needs if required

We do not sell your personal details.

5. Email delivery (EmailJS)

Contact form submissions may be delivered to our inbox using EmailJS (or a similar email relay configured for static hosting). That means your form fields are sent through their service so we can receive your message without running our own server. Please review EmailJS’s own privacy documentation for how they process transmission data. If EmailJS is not yet configured, we ask you to contact us by email or phone instead.

6. Cookies, analytics, and similar technologies

At launch, this website may use limited third-party cookies or local storage through embedded services. When we enable Google Analytics (or a similar analytics tool), it may collect aggregated traffic data such as pages visited and approximate location. We will use that information only to understand website performance and improve content.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect map or font loading on certain devices.

7. Third-party services and embeds

This website may load content or resources from third parties, including:

  • Google Fonts — for typography
  • Google Maps — map embed on the Contact page
  • Video hosts (for example YouTube or similar) — if a promotional video player is connected later
  • EmailJS — enquiry form delivery
  • GitHub Pages (or our hosting provider) — to serve the static website

These providers process data under their own policies. We encourage you to review their terms if you want detail about their processing.

8. Data retention

We keep enquiry messages only as long as needed to respond and for reasonable business follow-up, unless a longer period is required by law. If you want us to delete or update contact details you sent us, email info@cleanvo.in with enough information for us to locate your request.

9. Data security

We take practical steps to protect information sent to us. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security of data sent through the website or email.

10. Children

This website is intended for a general adult audience interested in home hygiene products and business enquiries. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for marketing purposes through this site.

11. Your choices

Depending on applicable law, you may ask us to:

  • Confirm whether we hold enquiry information you sent
  • Correct inaccurate contact details
  • Delete enquiry information where we are not required to keep it

Contact us at info@cleanvo.in for privacy requests.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do. Continued use of the website after an update means you should review the revised policy.
