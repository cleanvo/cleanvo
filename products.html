Floor Cleaner (Phenyl)

Lavender Fresh · Net qty 1 L

Cleaning Made Better.

A practical everyday floor cleaner for homes that want clean floors and a fresh finish — without a complicated routine. Dilute in a bucket, mop as usual, and follow the guidance on your pack.

Clean floors
Fresh fragrance
Easy to use
Suitable for most floors

How to use

1

Dilute

Add the recommended amount to a bucket of water as directed on the pack.

2

Mop

Mop the floor evenly so the surface is cleaned as part of your normal routine.

3

No rinse needed

Allow the floor to dry. No separate rinse step for regular use — follow pack instructions.

Fragrance note: Lavender Fresh is chosen for a clean, pleasant finish after mopping — not an overpowering perfume.

Important: Always read the label on your bottle for directions, storage, and caution. Pack guidance is the authority for safe use.
