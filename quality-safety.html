Quality & Safety

Clear guidance for everyday use. Your product pack label is the final authority — this page helps you use Cleanvo products carefully and confidently.

Cleanvo Care Solution builds home hygiene products for real households. We keep communication simple: how to use, how to store, and what to watch for — without exaggerated claims.

Safe usage Storage Packaging Guidelines Caution Quality promise

Safe usage

Use each product only for its intended household purpose, and follow the directions printed on the bottle or pack.

  • Read the label before first use — dilution, contact time, and surface notes matter.
  • Keep products away from children and pets. Store bottles upright and closed after use.
  • Do not mix Cleanvo products with other cleaners or chemicals unless the pack says it is safe.
  • Use in a ventilated area when the pack recommends it.
  • If irritation occurs, stop use, rinse the affected area with water, and seek medical advice if needed. Take the pack with you.

Storage instructions

Good storage protects product quality and household safety. Align your home habits with the storage notes on your pack.

Keep cool & dry

Store away from direct sunlight, heat sources, and damp floor areas when possible.

Original container

Keep product in its original bottle with the cap secured. Do not transfer to unmarked containers used for food or drink.

Away from food

Store separately from food items, cooking utensils, and drinking water storage.

Check the date

Use within the period indicated by manufacturing and expiry details on your pack.

Packaging standards

Our packaging is designed to be clear and useful at the point of use — product name, net quantity, directions, caution, and manufacturer details where required.

  • Labels include usage steps and key caution notes in plain language.
  • Net quantity and batch/date fields support traceability on physical packs.
  • Recycle guidance on packs should be followed according to your local rules.
  • If a label is damaged or unreadable, do not guess — contact us before use.

Customer guidelines

A few practical habits that help every household:

  • Test on a small hidden area if you are unsure about a sensitive surface.
  • For Floor Cleaner, dilute as directed before mopping; more product is not always better.
  • Upcoming products (dishwash, toilet cleaner, hand wash, white phenyl) will each carry their own pack instructions — use those when they launch.
  • Dealers and distributors should store cartons upright, dry, and away from extreme heat.
  • Questions about availability or usage? Reach our team via the Contact page.

Caution highlights

Cleaning products are for household use as directed. Treat them with the same care you would any home hygiene product.

  • For external household cleaning use only — not for consumption.
  • Avoid contact with eyes. In case of contact, rinse thoroughly with water.
  • Wash hands after handling concentrated product where the pack advises it.

Special note on toilet cleaners

When Cleanvo Toilet Cleaner launches, follow only the directions and caution printed on that pack. Acid-based bathroom cleaners require careful handling — never mix with other cleaners (including bleach), and keep the pack label as your guide. This website does not replace on-pack instructions.
